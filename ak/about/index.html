    About this site and myself

    Being graduated as a Physicist Engineer some time ago I almost immediately left the trail of physics and have been developing electronics and embedded software ever since.

    Through the years a number of interesting projects and mere anecdotes, encouraging and inspiring articles and books, clever tricks, nuances, and weird corner cases exceeded my internal storage capacity. What has leaked out of it and I dislike losing is captured on these pages.

    Subjects of interest are programmable logic, Systems-on-Chip and Systems-on-Module, embedded hardware and software, and anything else I feel fancy to rumble about – there are no upfront limits, but do not expect topics to divert too far away.

    Occasionally posts from this blog may be published on other online resources. In such cases, this site remains the original and authoritative source.
